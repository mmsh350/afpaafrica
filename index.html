About AFPA

The Accredited Financial Planners' Association is a unique professional association for practicing and prospective financial planners especially those interested in practicing within the African continent. The Association is established to promote the financial planning profession through quality assured training and certification of those fit to practice as Accredited Financial Planners (AFP).

Dr. Abdullahi Ya'u Usman,
PhD, AFP, FCFA. FCNA, FAGFM, FCIWM, CRA
President in Council

You are welcome to the world of financial planners. The Accredited Financial Planners Association (AFPA) is a leading professional body that promotes deeper penetration of professional practice in the African economy while setting pace in the development of sound curriculum for training and certification as a means of ensuring quality service and client/employer satisfaction.ding establishment of country, regional, bloc, and state chapters within the continent, as well as promoting private practice of financial planning by qualified individuals and firms through its innovative licensing scheme. Market penetration efforts are also made in the direction of supporting professional and academic institutions enhance their relevant curriculum to capture important aspects of financial planning and financial literacy.

Mission 

To support widespread recognition and application of financial planning skills in financial and investment decision making processes taken by individuals, corporations and governments in the African continent. We aim at enhancing the capacity of financial services professionals such as to positively impact on the economy at both macro and micro levels towards realizing socio-economic cultures that will promote optimum utilization of the Continentâ€™s resources.

Vision 

We strive to deliver a financial services profession that will be a core player in ensuring personal, corporate and government financial excellence through breeding the most ethically reliable, technically dependable and professionally objective financial planners in Africa and across the world.

Philosophy 

The core philosophy behind the establishment of AFPA revolves around the desire to be an indigenous and continental leader in the provision of quality and innovative financial planning education and practice.

Core Values 

At AFPA, we value everything good for the humankind. In particular, we care for the environment, our neighborhoods, the economy, the client, the employer and character of need to provide our services genuinely and wholesomely.

We have strategically developed an accredited curriculum that will deliver you to the beautiful world of financial planning practice, as the whole world need financial planners especially in the post Covid-19 pandemic period.
