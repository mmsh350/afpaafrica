MINISTRY OF INTERIOR

CITIZENSHIP AND BUSINESS DEPARTMENT


IMPORTANT ANNOUNCEMENT

1. This is to inform all Public Places of Worship that have applied for Renewal of their License to conduct statutory marriages that the Permanent Secretary/Principal Registrar of Marriages has approved a "Six (6) month stay of action (moratorium) on the expiration of their licenses."

2. All places of worship that had applied for the renewal of their licenses now have a grace period of six months to continue with the conduct of marriages pending their inspection.  Upon successful completion of the inspection and payment of the appropriate inspection fees, final approval shall be granted.

3. Consequently, all applicants for marriage can now access the eCitiBiz portal to choose their places of worship for the celebration of marriage within a six month period with effect from the 15th of February, 2021.     

4. Thank you for your usual cooperation.

SIGNED: PERMANENT SECRETARY/
PRINCIPAL REGISTRAR OF MARRIAGES
MINISTRY OF INTERIOR

eCitibiz - MARRIAGE RE-CERTIFICATION

This is applicable to couples who got married not in line with the provision of the Marriage Act (2004) and wish to obtain the Federal Marriage Certificate in accordance to the MARRIAGE ACT, CAP M6, VOLUME 8, LAW OF THE FEDERATION OF NIGERIA (2004). The re-certification of your marriage certificate would grant you the following
  • Recognition of your marriage under the law, MARRIAGE ACT, CAP M6, VOLUME 8, LAW OF THE FEDERATION OF NIGERIA (2004).
  • Express recognition of your marriage certificate for Embassy Visa interviews and Immigration purposes.
  • Identification number of your marriage certificate secured in the National Database.
  • National Identification number per couple
  • Recognition of your marriage certificate to access Federal Government of Nigeria services
The cost per couple for this service is ₦7,500

eCitibiz - WORSHIP RE-CERTIFICATION

This Re-Certification is applicable to all Places of Worship previously licensed by the Local Government Area (LGA) in Nigeria. With this Re-Certification your Place of Worship will now be licensed by the Ministry of Interior to conduct Legal marriages in line with the Marriage Act, CAP M6 Law of the Federation of Nigeria (2004) of the Constitution of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.

The cost per Place of Worship is ₦3O,000
